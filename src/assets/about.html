Let's break this down, starting with the moneyline bet.

What does -140 or +120 mean?
-140 on the Celtics means that you must pay $140 to bet on the Celtics, and if they win, you win $100.
+120 on the Heat means that you must pay $100 to bet on the Heat, and if they win, you win $120.

Ok great. If you like to think in terms of probabilities like we do, we can turn these numbers into % chances to win.

If you were to take the -140 price on the Celtics, you are saying you think the Celtics have >= 58.33% chance to win.
If you were to take the +120 price on the Heat, you are saying you think the Celtics have <= 54.54% chance to win.

Something seems off though. What if you think the Celtics only have a 52% chance to win? With the prices offered by another site, you don't want to make any trade.
On Fortuna, you may create a new market between you and your groups.
